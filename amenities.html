Our Amenities

Radha Pandharinath

The beach is delightful to sit in overlooking the River-Ethope. Cool down in the crystal clear pool, enjoy marvels of Abraka.
ISKCON Pandharpur, Abraka, Delta State, Nigeria, provides you with “McCarthy Beach” – the place of choice for your vacations, picnics, weekend getaways, games, parties, outdoor social and corporate events, etc.
Swim or be sailed in our live boat around the safe waters of River Ethiope. Feel the difference natural and artificial waters.
Enjoy a very chilled bottle of bear or soft drink at our beach bar as you are caressed by the soft-cool wind of a natural river.
Visit our Barbeque and Hot Sausage Beach Spot, Phosky's Cocktails and get taste of the good life. The aroma, and taste will blow your mind and the taste shall last forever. Do you need to heat-up before diving into the beach? No worries, we have several beach games such as volleyball, football, paintball, and so much more.

Govindas

ISKCON Govinda's is a renowned vegetarian restaurant chain affiliated with the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON). Known for its delectable fusion of Indian and international cuisine, this dining establishment not only tantalizes the taste buds but also provides a unique spiritual experience. Located in or near ISKCON temples, the restaurants offer a tranquil and devotional ambiance, making them ideal for those seeking a peaceful and nourishing meal. With warm hospitality, cultural events, and a diverse menu, ISKCON Govinda's caters to a broad spectrum of visitors, uniting food, spirituality, and community in one harmonious setting.

Conference Hall

Go Shala

Brahmanya Dev (loving towards Brahmanas), Benefactor of cows and Brahmins and Ever well wisher of the world. ( Padma Purana - Uttarakhand -229.9 )

Śrīla Prabhupada said - " The cow is the most important animal for developing the human body to perfection. The body can be maintained by any kind of foodstuff, but cow's milk is particularly essential for developing the finer tissues of the human brain so that one can understand the intricacies of transcendental knowledge." Your generous donations will enable us to provide a loving and secure environment for our cows By supporting Go Seva, you play a crucial role in ensuring that these noble creatures receive proper nourishment, medical care and shelter. Your contributions will help us maintain our goshala (cow shelter) facilities, procure nutritious fodder and offer essential veterinary service

Prashad

ISKCON's Food for Life is an inspiring initiative that embraces the principles of love, compassion, and unity.
Through the distribution of sanctified vegetarian meals, the organization strives to address hunger and nourish both the body and the soul.
Its dedicated volunteers and supporters work tirelessly to bring hope and sustenance to those in need, fostering a sense of community and kindness in the process.
