About ISKCON Pandharpur Guest House

ISKCON, or the International Society for Krishna Consciousness, is a global spiritual organization following the teachings of Lord Krishna and the path of bhakti yoga.
Founded by A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada in 1966, it promotes devotion to Krishna through activities like public chanting and managing temples and guest houses, such as the ISKCON Pandharpur Guest House.
These guest houses offer both comfort and a spiritually enriching environment for pilgrims and travelers, enhancing their experience at sacred destinations like Pandharpur.

Kalyan Mandhap

ISKCON Kalyan Mandap, located in the serene surroundings of Kalyan, Maharashtra, offers a unique blend of modern convenience and natural beauty.
With both air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned rooms, a spacious and flexible event hall, and a budget-friendly dormitory, it caters to a wide range of preferences and budgets. The venue's tranquil ambiance, ample parking, and scenic backdrops make it perfect for weddings, receptions, corporate events, and cultural celebrations.
With professional staff and catering services, ISKCON Kalyan Mandap ensures that your event is a memorable and hassle-free experience.
