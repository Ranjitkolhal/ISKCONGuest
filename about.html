About Guest House

Pandharpur Dhama is the beautiful holy abode of Lord Vithala, Sri Krishna Himself. Located in Southeastern Maharashtra, Pandharpur Dhama is the spiritual headquarters of Maharashtrian bhaktas.
Sri Chandrabhaga River usually gently curves around the town of Pandharpur in a crescent half moon shape, thus her name Chandrabhaga which means "half moon".
The worship of Vitthal in the Pandharpur temple is based mainly and the contri of the Vaishnav saints of Maharashtra and Karnataka during the the 13th through the 17th centuries, namely, Dnyaneshwar, Namdev, Eknath, Tukaram, Purandar Das, Vijay Das, Gopal Das, and Jagannath Das, have augmented the worship..The Pandharpur temple covers a large area, and has six gates. The eastern gate is known as the "Namdev Gate.
ISKCON Pandharpur is a part of the larger ISKCON movement, which aims to promote the teachings of Lord Krishna as revealed in the ancient scriptures like the Bhagavad Gita and the Srimad Bhagavatam. ISKCON temples emphasize the practice of bhakti yoga, the path of devotional service, as the means to attain spiritual growth and a deeper connection with Krishna. Devotees at ISKCON Pandharpur engage in various activities, including congregational chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra, offering prayers and arati (devotional rituals), studying scriptures, and participating in festivals and celebrations. The temple also conducts regular discourses, seminars, and cultural programs to educate and inspire the visitors.
